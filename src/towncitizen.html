Yueyao Wang 王乐瑶

Yueyao Wang

She is the town's musician, skilled at playing the xiao (Chinese flute). Her cheerful personality is matched by her sweet smile. She particularly enjoys performing in gardens, harboring an endless passion for music. Her life is filled with melody and joy, and she takes pride in bringing happiness to people during festival celebrations.

Favorite Thing: Forest Walk

Zhiyuan Wen 文志远

Zhiyuan Wen

Wen Zhiyuan is the town's recognized scholar, known for both his vast knowledge and lofty ambitions. His composed demeanor reflects his scholarly character. He spends most of his life in his study, immersed in books, constantly pursuing knowledge and wisdom.

Favorite Thing: Discussing Politics

Xinyue Tong 童心悦

Xinyue Tong

She is the town's beloved little troublemaker, famous for her carefree and energetic spirit. With her bright personality and endless curiosity about the world, she loves running through every street and alley in town, exploring every corner. Nothing makes her happier than listening to her grandmother's stories.

Favorite Thing: Flying a Kite

Weihu Huo 霍卫虎

Weihu Huo

He serves as the town guard, known for his easy-going nature. While sometimes careless in his actions, he never hesitates to step up when it matters most to keep the town safe. His life is simple – when not patrolling or training, he enjoys spending time with the town's children, teaching them basic self-defense moves.

Favorite Thing: After-work Drinks

Wudie Zhou 周舞蝶

Wudie Zhou

She is a graceful dancer who moves like a fairy, dressed in a flowing green gown, holding long silk ribbons, and wearing a gentle smile. Her gentle nature draws her to seek dance inspiration from nature, always pursuing a harmonious blend between her movements and the natural world.

Favorite Thing: Cats and Rabbits
